Welcome to BOSS.

Description

BOSS is a tool for modeling stochastic reactions. Over the course of five weeks, our PGSS team created BOSS using Python. We implemented the Gillespie Algorithm to realistically model the relationships of various natural species with each other. By taken into account the factor of randomness and natural fluctuations, our model proves to be more accurate than a model that merely uses Ordinary Differential Equations (ODEs).
